Scripture Union

Scripture Union Rwanda is an interdenominational christian organisation operating in Rwanda since 1969. The main objective of the movement is to help Christians at different ages to grow spiritually, and the channel used for this growth is the daily reading of the Bible, Camps and Scripture engagement.

Two departments work together to achieve this main objective:

      Biblical Ministry
      Family Ministry ( Children, Youth and Couples ministries )
