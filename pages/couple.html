Couple Ministry

The couple ministry organize couple's meetings in which people share marital wisdom teachings and other marital related experiences and pray together. Enjoy your marriage and positive parenting seminars are organized to strengthen families.

Hope for Orphans and Widows

Widows in local committees have been identified and prayer groups have been launched within the context of healing their wounds, sharing the messages of hope and strengthening one another. Dynamic groups have gone up to organizing local Bible camps and even launching small income generating intiatives where they work in associations. The spiritual care of the widows has brought SU to the assistance of orphans. Schools fees are provided to children from poor families head by widows.
